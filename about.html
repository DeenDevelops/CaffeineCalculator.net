About

The Caffeine Calculator was created to raise awareness of our daily caffeine consumption. It is estimated that approximately 80% of the world's population consumes a caffeinated product every day. Therefore, managing your caffeine intake responsibly is crucial for maintaining good health. When you're healthier, you're happier, and when you're happier, you can share that happiness with others. -Deen
